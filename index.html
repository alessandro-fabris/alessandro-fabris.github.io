I am a postodoctoral researcher at Max Planck Institute for Security and Privacy working with Asia Biega and supported by the FINDHR project on fairness and non-discrimination in algorithmic hiring.

I study algorithmic fairness and auditing in information access systems. My research centers on the definition, operationalization, and measurement of fairness, with a focus on domain-specific requirements, and a critical perspective on data management and ethics.

Previously, I worked four years in the industry with IBM and Electrolux. To develop relevant solutions for pressing socio-technical issues, it is key to guide and translate policy into resposible computing practices that can be understood and adopted by practitioners.
